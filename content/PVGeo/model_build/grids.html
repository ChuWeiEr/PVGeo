Create Tensor Mesh¶

class PVGeo.model_build.grids.CreateTensorMesh(origin=[-350.0, -400.0, 0.0], data_name='Data', xcellstr='200 100 50 20*50.0 50 100 200', ycellstr='200 100 50 21*50.0 50 100 200', zcellstr='20*25.0 50 100 200')[source]¶
